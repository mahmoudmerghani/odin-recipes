Homemade French Fries

Description

These Homemade French Fries are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside, perfect for satisfying your cravings. They're easy to make and much tastier than store-bought fries!

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes, peeled and cut into sticks
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Rinse the potato sticks in cold water to remove excess starch. Pat dry thoroughly with paper towels.
  3. Carefully add the potato sticks to the hot oil in batches, frying until golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  4. Remove fries from the oil and drain on paper towels. Season with salt immediately.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Homemade French Fries!
