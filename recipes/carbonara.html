Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a traditional Italian pasta dish made with a creamy egg sauce, crispy pancetta, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. This comforting and indulgent meal is perfect for a quick and delicious dinner.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 4 oz pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat with the pancetta and garlic.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring continuously until the eggs thicken and create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
