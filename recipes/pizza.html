Pizza

Description

This classic pizza features a golden, crispy crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, gooey melted mozzarella cheese, and a sprinkle of fresh basil. The perfect balance of savory flavors makes each bite a delightful experience, ideal for pizza lovers of all ages. Enjoy it fresh out of the oven for the best taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Drizzle the olive oil over the cheese and sprinkle with dried oregano.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and scatter the fresh basil leaves on top.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then slice and serve hot.
